About the Event

TANTRAZ 2025 is a dynamic and innovative technical and non-technical fest designed to ignite creativity, problem-solving, and collaboration among tech enthusiasts and creative minds. Hosted by School of Computing, VelTech University, this festival is a platform where students showcase their technical prowess, innovative ideas, and artistic skills through a variety of exciting competitions.
